WordPress Sheffield

A WordPress meet-up for developers, designers and WordPress enthusiasts.

2nd Tuesday of every month at The Workstation, Sheffield.

Sounds pretty good right?

Why not offer to speak or just sign up for FREE!

What?

A monthly meet-up and social gathering where we talk about WordPress as a web design and development platform.

Topics include themes, frameworks, plugins, BuddyPress, making plugins and developing applications, hacking, working with clients, info on the latest beta and release versions and anything else WP-related.

Events include presentations, Q&A's, discussions, rants, problem solving and general banter and chatter.

Each event is approximately 2 hours long followed by a few hours in a local bar.

Who?

The session is chaired by UK-WordPress rockstar and all round nice guy Kimb Jones (he wrote this himself).

The space is provided by the lovely people at The GIST Foundation with special thanks to Jag Goraya for his tireless work in making the event happen.

Where?

The main event takes place in the wonderful Workstation, Sheffield. Which is just a short walk from the train station. The venue has plenty of room, a projector, tea and coffee facilities and, if you're lucky, beer!

After the session the group tend to hit a local bar, these are normally either The Rutland Arms, The Showroom Bar or The Sheffield Tap (at the train station).

Can't make the full session? Just follow the hashtag and find us in the bar!

Why?

Because we love WordPress! More specifically, we love talking about WordPress, moaning about WordPress and using WordPress to build our websites and applications.

And because WordPress is open source, free and has a strong-UK community of committed developers, designers and hackers.

So if you use WordPress and are near Sheffield then come along, satisfaction is guaranteed :)
