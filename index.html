WordPress Sheffield

A free monthly WordPress meetup for developers, designers, bloggers and WordPress enthusiasts.

2nd Tuesday of every month at The Showroom, Sheffield.

WordPress Sheffield Re-Launch Party!

After a two month sabbatical WordPress Sheffield will is BACK with a re-launch party at 6:30pm on Tuesday September 10th at the Cafe Bar in the Showroom, Sheffield.

We'll have a packed roster of top speakers and sessions include: 'Converting to WordPress Self Hosted', 'Simple Git for WordPress theme designers', 'A tour of the new WordPress dashboard and MP6', 'What we learned on the #WPUK tour' and 'A look ahead to WordCamp Europe and WordCamp London'.

Tickets are FREE (as usual) and food will be provided so sign-up now!

Meetup Structure & Topics

Talks & Presentations

Sessions from industry professionals on WP-related topics.

Show & Tells

Participants are encouraged to show off their projects.

Friendly Chat & Banter

Q&As discussions, rants, problem solving and general chatter.

Topics include themes, frameworks, plugins, BuddyPress, making plugins and developing applications, hacking, working with clients, info on the latest beta and release versions and anything else WP-related.

You can follow Lanyrd for info on upcoming sessions and we recommend you sign up to the mailing list by hitting the big button below.

Hosted in the centre of Sheffield

The Showroom

The main event takes place in the wonderful Workstation, Sheffield. Which is just a short walk from the train station. The venue has plenty of room, a projector, tea and coffee facilities and, if you're lucky, beer!

The after party...

After the session the group tend to hit a local bar, these are normally either next door in The Showroom Bar, up the road at The Rutland Arms or down at the train station in The Sheffield Tap

Can't make the full session? Just follow the hashtag and find us in the bar!

Who runs this?

The session is chaired by UK-WordPress rockstar and all round nice guy Kimb Jones (he wrote this himself). You can follow Kimb on twitter for announcements of upcoming events, topics and other general WordPress goings on or sign up to his mailing list.

Other regular attendees include Matt Watson, Ian Parr, Tim Nash (who organises WordPress Leeds), Simon Dunton and the Human Made team.

Sessions require sponsorship - this is currently provided by by the lovely people at Make Do (Kimb & Matt) and was previously lovingly provided by The GIST Foundation.
